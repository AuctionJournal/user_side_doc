# How can an auctioneer use the mailing list in Customers? What is the benefit?

**Mailing lists** let you **group customers (clients)** under your auction company so you can organize contacts and **export** them for communication. Lists live under **Customers** in the Auctioneer Dashboard, alongside your client list.

---

## What is the benefit?

| Benefit | What it helps you do |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Organize contacts** | Split clients into named groups (for example past buyers, consignors, a sale mailing). |
| **Add or remove members anytime** | Keep each list up to date as people join or leave your CRM. |
| **Export to CSV** | Download a list’s members (name, code, phone, email) for use in your email tool or other outreach. |
| **See who is in a list** | Open a list to review members and search within it. |
| **Tag clients when you add them** | When you create or edit a client, you can assign **mailing lists** on step 1. |
| **Website subscribers** | Auction Journal maintains a protected list **Subscribed From Website** for people who subscribe on your public presence (you cannot delete that list). |

Mailing lists are **groups inside Auction Journal**—they help you manage **who** belongs to **which** audience. Sending email from the dashboard through a list is not the main workflow today; **export** is how most auctioneers use a list with external mail tools.

---

## Open mailing lists

1. Sign in to the **Auctioneer Dashboard**.  
2. Open **Customers**.  
3. Switch to **MAILING LIST** (toggle at the top on desktop, or the selector on mobile).  

You can also go directly to the mailing-list screen; the route is under **Customers** → **Mailing list**.

From **CLIENT LIST**, choose **MAILING LIST** to switch views.

---

## Create a mailing list

1. On the **Mailing list** screen, select **Add new MailingList**.  
2. Enter a **name** (at least 5 characters) and **description** (at least 5 characters).  
3. Save.

The new list appears in the table with **Name**, **Description**, and **Member count**.

---

## Work with a list’s members

See [How do I manage customers in a mailing list?](mailing-list-members.md) for the full flow on `/dashboard/clients/mailinglists/view` — **Add Client**, checkboxes, **Add All** / **Remove All**, **Save**, and single-row remove.

**Export:** On the mailing-list home row or list view, use **Export** for a CSV of members.

**Member count** includes both linked **clients** and any **email-only** entries (website subscribers on the system list).

---

## Assign lists when adding a client

When you **add or edit** a client manually (step 1 of the client form), use **Select Mailing List** to attach them to one or more lists at save time. You can change membership later from the list **View** screen as well.

---

## Subscribed From Website (system list)

- Created automatically for your company when someone subscribes via the website flow.  
- Shown in your mailing lists with a name like **Subscribed From Website**.  
- You **cannot delete** this list (delete is disabled in the UI).  
- It may include **clients** matched by email and **standalone emails** that are not yet full client records.

---

## Tips

- Use **search** on the mailing-list home page to find a list by name.  
- Use a clear **description** so staff know who belongs in each list.  
- Export before a big mail merge so you work from a current file.
