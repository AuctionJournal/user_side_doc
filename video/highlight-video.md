# How do I highlight my videos in Auction Journal?

To give a **video** more visibility on the public site, create a **Video Ad** under **Advertise your Business**—not a separate highlight control on the video library page.

## What to do

1. Add and publish your video first ([add video content](add-content.md)).
2. Open **Advertise With Us** in your dashboard (`/dashboard/advertisement`).
3. Choose **Advertise your Business**.
4. Select the **Video Ad** package (price/duration option).
5. Select the **video** you want to promote from your list.
6. Choose the **run dates** for the ad.
7. Complete **payment** at checkout.

After payment, the video is promoted on Auction Journal for your selected dates according to the Video Ad package.

## Good to know

- Your video should exist in your content library before you can select it in the ad wizard.
- Video ads are **paid** campaigns by duration. See [How does Advertisement work?](../advertisement/how-it-works.md).
