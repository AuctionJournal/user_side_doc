# How do I find helpful videos in Auction Journal?

Anyone can watch videos on the **public website** [auctionjournal.com](https://auctionjournal.com)—you do not need to sign in. Videos are posted by **auctioneers** on Auction Journal and by **Auction Journal** itself. They are helpful if you **bid**, follow auctions, or want tips, tutorials, and auction insights before or after a sale.

This works like reading [blogs on Auction Journal](../blog/find-blogs.md): public content for learning; bidding still requires registering for each auction separately.

---

## Open the videos page

1. Go to [auctionjournal.com](https://auctionjournal.com).  
2. In the main menu, select **VIDEOS**.

You land on the **videos** page: [auctionjournal.com/videos](https://auctionjournal.com/videos).

**From the home page:** Scroll to **RECOMMENDED VIDEOS** and select **View All**—that also opens the videos page.

---

## What you see on the videos page

The page has two main areas:

| Section | What it is |
|---------|------------|
| **Editor’s pick** | A carousel at the top with highlighted videos (up to a few slides). Watch in place; each slide shows the **title** and **Posted by** (who published it). |
| **Video library** | A grid of more videos below, **nine per page**. Use **pagination** at the bottom if there are more than nine. |

Each video card shows:

- A **preview** you can play (YouTube embed)  
- **Title**  
- **Posted by** — the auctioneer company or Auction Journal

Select the play control on a card to watch that video in the listing.

---

## Who posts these videos?

| Source | What you get |
|--------|----------------|
| **Auctioneers** on Auction Journal | Sale previews, how-to clips, company updates |
| **Auction Journal** | Platform tips and general auction content |

You do not need a bidder account to watch. To participate in a live or timed sale, use **auctions** on the site and complete **registration** for that event.

---

## Quick tips

- Start from **Editor’s pick** for staff-chosen highlights, then browse the full grid.  
- Use **pagination** to see older videos—newer items are usually on earlier pages depending on sort order.  
- Check **Posted by** if you want content from a specific auction company you follow.  
- For articles and news, use [How do I find helpful blogs?](../blog/find-blogs.md) on the **BLOGS** menu.
