# How do I create a print, poster, or banner advertisement?

Under **Advertise your Business**, these ad types let you **upload your own creative** (images and links) instead of selecting an existing listing.

---

## National Print Ad

1. **Advertise With Us** → **Advertise your Business**.
2. Choose **National Print Ad** and a **package** (price/days).
3. Fill in the print form: **title**, **images**, **link**, and other required fields.
4. Set **run dates**, pay at checkout, and wait for **approval** before it appears on the public site.

---

## Poster ads (national or state)

| Package | Extra step |
|---------|------------|
| **National Poster Ad** | Upload poster image + link |
| **State Poster Ad** | Same + **choose state** |

Posters are image-based promotions shown in poster placements on the public site.

---

## Banner ads (national or state)

| Package | Extra step |
|---------|------------|
| **National Banner Ad** | Upload banner image + link |
| **State Banner Ad** | Same + **choose state** |

Banners use wide image placements suitable for hero-style areas.

---

## Tips

- Use clear, high-resolution images and a working **destination link**.
- Match **national vs state** to where you want bidders to see the ad ([national vs state](national-vs-state.md)).
- Plan **start and end dates** around your auction marketing window ([pricing and duration](pricing-and-duration.md)).
