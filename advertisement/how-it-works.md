# How does Advertisement work in Auction Journal? How can an auctioneer benefit from it?

**Advertise With Us** lets you pay to promote your auctions, listings, blogs, and videos on the public Auction Journal website. Promoted content is shown in featured areas so more bidders see it first.

Open the flow from your dashboard: **Advertise With Us** (`/dashboard/advertisement`).

---

## What you are buying

You choose:

1. **What to promote** — mainly **Listings** or **Business** content (blog/video/banner/poster/print).
2. **Ad type** — for example ticker, featured listing (national/state), banner, poster, print, blog ad, video ad.
3. **How long it runs** — packages are priced by **duration** (number of days in the selected date range).
4. **Where it shows** — **National** ads appear on broad site pages; **State** ads appear on that state's listing/auction views.

All advertisement packages are **paid**. After checkout, your request is **submitted**; the ad goes **live on the public site after approval**, for the dates you selected.

---

## How it helps your business

- **More visibility** — your listing or brand appears in featured sections, not only in the regular list.
- **Faster discovery** — national placement puts you in front of visitors on main discovery pages.
- **Local focus** — state ads target bidders browsing a specific state.
- **Flexible campaigns** — pick start and end dates to match your auction marketing window.
- **Supports full marketing mix** — promote listings plus business assets (blogs, videos, banners).

---

## Simple flow (auctioneer view)

1. Go to **Advertise With Us**.
2. Pick a category (for example **Advertise your Listings** or **Advertise your Business**).
3. Choose an ad package and price/duration option.
4. Select or build the content to promote.
5. Choose run dates (and state, if it is a state ad).
6. Complete **payment** at checkout.
7. Your request is reviewed; when **approved**, the ad appears on the public website for your selected dates.

See [When ads go live](go-live.md).

---

## National vs state placement (plain language)

| Scope | Where bidders usually see it |
|-------|------------------------------|
| **National** | High-traffic pages across the site (home and national featured areas) |
| **State** | Pages filtered to that state for listings/auctions |

---

## Good to know

- **Advertise your Auctions** may appear in the category list but can be unavailable/disabled until that product is released—use **Listings** and **Business** for current campaigns.
- You need a **payment card on file** for checkout (see [Payment card](../auctioneeer/payment-method.md)).
- If an ad does not show yet, confirm **payment**, **approval**, and that your selected dates include today.
