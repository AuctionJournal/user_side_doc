# How do I advertise my blog or video content?

Promote content you already added in Auction Journal — you **select** the blog or video, you do not upload new creative in the ad builder.

---

## Blogs Ad

1. Go to **Advertise With Us** → **Advertise your Business**.
2. Choose **Blogs Ad** and pick a **package**.
3. Select one of your **blogs** from the list.
4. Choose **run dates**, complete **checkout**.
5. After **approval**, your blog can appear in **featured blog** areas on the public site.

Your blog should already meet Auction Journal content rules ([blog approval](../blog/approval-and-rejection.md)).

---

## Video Ad

1. Same path: **Advertise your Business** → **Video Ad** → package.
2. Select a **video** you published in your dashboard.

![Select video for video ad](../image/advertisement/video-ad-select.png "Select video")

3. Set dates, pay, wait for **approval** for featured video placement.

See [video approval](../video/approval-and-rejection.md) for content rules.
