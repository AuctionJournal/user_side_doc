# What types of Advertisement are available to auctioneers in Auction Journal?

Open **Advertise With Us** (`/dashboard/advertisement`) and choose a **category**, then an **ad type** and **package** (price per duration).

![Select advertisement category](../image/advertisement/select-category.png "Select category")

*Step 1: Advertise your Listings, Advertise your Auctions, or Advertise your Business.*

---

## Advertise your Listings

Promote a **published listing** you already created.

| Ad type | What it does (summary) |
|---------|-------------------------|
| **Ticker Symbol** | Listing message in the **site header** ticker |
| **Featured Listing (National)** | Listing shown in **national** featured areas |
| **Featured Listing (State)** | Listing featured on that **state’s** listing pages |

![Listing boost packages](../image/advertisement/listing-boost-packages.png "Listing ad packages")

*Example packages: Ticker, Featured National, Featured State with price/duration options.*

You **select** the listing (you do not build new creative in this step). See [Listing ads](listing-ads.md).

---

## Advertise your Auctions

This category appears on the first screen but is **not available** yet in the dashboard. Use **Listings** or **Business** for current campaigns.

---

## Advertise your Business

Mix of **create-your-own** ads and **select existing** blog/video content.

| Ad type | You typically |
|---------|----------------|
| **National Print Ad** | Upload print creative (title, images, link, schedule fields) |
| **National Poster Ad** | Upload poster image + link |
| **State Poster Ad** | Poster + choose **state** |
| **National Banner Ad** | Upload banner image + link |
| **State Banner Ad** | Banner + choose **state** |
| **Blogs Ad** | Select an existing **blog** |
| **Video Ad** | Select an existing **video** |

![Business advertisement packages](../image/advertisement/business-packages.png "Business ad packages")

*Business category package list.*

Details: [Print, poster, and banner](business-create-ads.md) · [Blog and video ads](blog-video-ads.md)
