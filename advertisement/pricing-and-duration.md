# How does advertisement pricing and duration work?

Every advertisement is sold as a **package**: a fixed **price** for a fixed number of **days** (or a date range up to that limit).

---

## How you see prices

On the package screen, each option shows something like:

**$99 / 7 days**

That means you pay **$99** for a package that runs up to **7 days** (or the day count shown on the card you select).

- Different ad types (ticker, featured, banner, etc.) have **different** package rows.
- You may see **multiple packages** per ad type (e.g. 7-day vs 14-day).

![Listing boost packages](../image/advertisement/listing-boost-packages.png "Package prices")

---

## Choosing run dates

After you select or build your ad content, you pick **start** and **end** dates.

![Select advertisement duration](../image/advertisement/select-duration.png "Duration picker")

- The package you bought sets how long the campaign can run.
- Your ad is scheduled for that window once it is **approved** and live.

---

## Checkout

At payment you see one **Advertisement** line with the price and your planned dates.

![Payment summary](../image/advertisement/payment-summary.png "Checkout")

Payment uses the same auctioneer billing flow as other paid features (invoices appear in your payment history).
