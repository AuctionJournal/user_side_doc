# How do I promote a listing (ticker vs featured national vs featured state)?

Open **Advertise With Us** → **Advertise your Listings**, then pick one of three ways to boost a **published listing**.

---

## Ticker Symbol

- **What it does:** Shows a short message about your listing in the **header ticker** on the public site.
- **What you do:** Select the **listing**; no custom banner design in this step.
- **Reach:** Site-wide header visibility (not the same as a large featured card).

![Select listing for listing ad](../image/advertisement/listing-ticker-select.png "Select listing")

---

## Featured Listing (National)

- **What it does:** Puts your listing in **national featured** areas so more visitors see it on main discovery pages.
- **What you do:** Select the listing, choose dates, pay, wait for **approval**.
- **Best when:** You want broad exposure across Auction Journal.

---

## Featured Listing (State)

- **What it does:** Features your listing on **that state’s** listing views.
- **What you do:** Select the listing, choose **run dates**, then **select the state**, pay, wait for approval.
- **Best when:** Your auction is in one state and you want local bidders.

---

## Which should I pick?

| Goal | Choose |
|------|--------|
| High-visibility header message | **Ticker Symbol** |
| Maximum reach nationwide | **Featured Listing (National)** |
| Focus one state market | **Featured Listing (State)** |

You need a **published** listing before any of these options work.
