Auction Journal — sample user help questions
================================================================

Questions only — no answers yet.

The app has two main user types. Questions below are split by who
would typically ask them: AUCTIONEER vs BIDDER.


################################################################################
PART A — AUCTIONEER PERSPECTIVE (you run sales, listings, and your business)
################################################################################


Account, profile, and listings
--------------------------------

Why can't I publish my listing even though I filled everything in?

What is "free listing eligibility" and how do I become eligible?

Why does my listing need a full address before it can go live on the map?


Users and team
-------------------

What can an user do on my behalf versus what only I can do?

How do I add or remove an user from my auctioneer account?


Clients and contacts
--------------------

What is a "client" in the system, and how is that different from a bidder?

When a bidder updates their name or photo, why do my client records change?

How do I use the mailing list with my clients without breaking consent rules?


Listings you create
--------------------

What is the difference between a draft listing and a live public listing?

What steps must pass before I can hit publish?

What happens to a listing after the auction date on it has passed?


Auctions you run
----------------

How do I know which bidders are registered vs only browsing?

How do registration approvals or declines work for my auction?

What is clerking, and where do I handle floor bids versus online bids?

Where do I see or manage settlement and expenses after the sale?


Live webcast and video (auctioneer side)
----------------------------------------

How do I start or manage a live webcast for an onsite auction?

What should I do if bidders say they cannot see or hear the stream?


Auction lots (auctioneer / clerk view)
--------------------------------------

How do lots relate to the catalog I showed bidders before the sale?

How do I open, hold, or sell a lot during the live sale?


Blog and content (if you publish)
---------------------------------

Where do I choose category and type when I publish a blog post?

Why is my post not showing where I expected on the website?


Advertisements
--------------

How do I buy or renew an advertisement slot?

How are ad payments different from listing fees or auction settlement?


Payments and money (auctioneer)
-------------------------------

What is Stripe Connect and why does the app ask me to complete it?

Where do payouts from sales or fees show up for my business?


Newsletter (if you send campaigns)
----------------------------------

How do I send a newsletter to my mailing list?

How do I see who unsubscribed?


Help and support (auctioneer)
-----------------------------

Should I request a callback or email when something breaks on sale day?

Who do I contact for billing or Stripe issues for my company account?


Settings and organization info (auctioneer)
-------------------------------------------

Where do I change branding, defaults, or organization details?

Where do I manage notification preferences for my auctioneer account?


################################################################################
PART B — BIDDER PERSPECTIVE (you browse, register, bid, and buy)
################################################################################


Account, verification, and profile
----------------------------------

I uploaded my ID — why am I still not "verified" on the platform?

Why does the app ask me to add a payment card before I am fully verified?

Do I need both front and back of my driver's license, or is one side enough?


Auction registration and access
-------------------------------

How do I register for an auction, and how long until I can bid?

Why does it say I'm registered but I still can't place a bid?

Can I cancel or withdraw my registration from an auction?

What happens after I register — what should I watch for in email or the app?


Listings you view (marketing pages)
------------------------------------

I see a "request callback" or "get a bid pass" — what is the difference?

Why does my friend see a different button on the same listing than I do?

I already clicked something on a listing — why won't the app let me do it again?


Bidding and lots
----------------

How does bidding on a lot work compared to bidding on the whole auction?

Why was my bid rejected or not accepted?

How do I add a lot to my watchlist, and what does that do?

If I win several lots, how does payment or pickup work together?


Live video and webcast (bidder)
-------------------------------

When an auction says it has video or webcast, what should I expect?

The stream won't load — what should I check before I call support?

Do I have to be verified before I can watch or bid during a live webcast?


Bidder score and reputation
---------------------------

What is my bidder score, and why did it go up or down?

Does my score affect whether I can bid or register?


Blog and news (bidder)
----------------------

I'm looking for an article — how do categories or types help me find it?


Payments and checkout (bidder)
------------------------------

Why do I need a card on file even if I only plan to pay later at pickup?

Where do I update or remove my saved payment methods?


Marketing email and newsletter (bidder)
---------------------------------------

How do I stop receiving marketing emails?

I unsubscribed but still got an email — why might that happen?


Help and support (bidder)
-------------------------

Should I request a callback or email when I have a problem with bidding or payment?

Who do I contact — the auction house or Auction Journal support?


Settings (bidder)
-----------------

Where do I change my profile, password, or notification preferences?


################################################################################
PART C — EITHER ROLE (same support channel, different account areas)
################################################################################

The app logged me out or says my session expired — what should I do?

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

Where do I find the privacy policy or terms I agreed to?
