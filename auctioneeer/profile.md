# How can I update my profile details? Which fields can I update?

After you register, you can keep your business information current from the **Auctioneer Dashboard**. Most profile text is under **MY PROFILE**; your **profile picture** is updated from the **photo on the sidebar**; your **password** is changed on the same profile page under **Account Info**.

---

## Open your profile

1. Sign in to the **Auctioneer Dashboard**.
2. In the sidebar, select **MY PROFILE**.
3. The page title is **MY PROFILE**. You will see **Profile Info** and **Account Info**.

---

## What you can update

### Profile Info (Edit Profile)

Select **Edit Profile**, change the fields below, then **Save & Update**.

| Field | Required? | Notes |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Auction Company** | Yes | Your company name as shown to bidders |
| **Firstname** / **Lastname** | Yes | Your name as the contact person |
| **Address** | Yes | Street address line (the line you type in the form) |
| **Phone Number** | Yes | U.S. format: **+1** followed by 10 digits (12 characters total, e.g. `+12125551234`) |
| **Email** | Yes | Use **lowercase** letters in the email address |
| **Website** | Yes | Full URL (e.g. `https://www.yourcompany.com`) |
| **Auctioneer Bio** | Yes | Short description for your public auctioneer presence |
| **Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn** | Optional | Full links to your pages; leave blank to clear |

**Shown when viewing (not on the edit form):** your address may also display **state** and **ZIP** from registration. To change **city, state, ZIP, or country**, contact support or follow whatever process your account team provides—those fields are set during [registration](registration.md), not on this edit screen.

**Not on this page:** auctioneer **license number**, **license document**, or **Auctioneer ID**—those come from signup and system assignment.

### Profile picture (sidebar)

1. On the left sidebar, select the **pencil icon** on your avatar.
2. In **Update Profile Picture**:
   - **Upload Profile Picture** or **Change Profile Picture** — choose an image file.
   - **Remove Profile Picture** — clears the photo if one is set.

### Password (Account Info)

1. On **MY PROFILE**, scroll to **Account Info**.
2. Select **Change Password**.
3. Enter **old password**, **new password**, and **re-enter new password**.
4. Select **Save**.

Your new password must be at least **8 characters** and include **uppercase**, **lowercase**, a **number**, and a **special character**. After a successful change, Auction Journal sends a confirmation email to your account email.

---

## What this profile is used for

- **Public pages** — Company name, bio, website, social links, and photo can appear on your auctioneer presence on Auction Journal.
- **Settlement and invoices** — Company branding on PDFs also relies on accurate profile and [invoice details](../auctioneer-misc/invoice-details.md).

Keep email and phone current so bidders and support can reach you.
