# Who is an auctioneer? What do they do in Auction Journal?

An **auctioneer** on Auction Journal is a **business** (auction company or licensed auction professional) that uses the platform to **run sales**—auctions, listings, and related services—for sellers and buyers.

If you are signing up as an auctioneer, you will use the **Auctioneer Dashboard** (a separate sign-in from bidders). The public Auction Journal website is where your sales are discovered; the dashboard is where you build and manage them.

---

## Who is an auctioneer?

| Idea | In plain terms |
|------|----------------|
| **Who** | The auction **company** and the people who operate it on Auction Journal. |
| **Account** | One auctioneer business account per company (with your company name, license, contact details, and branding). |
| **Not a bidder account** | Bidders have their own registration and dashboard. Some people may work with both roles in real life, but on Auction Journal they are **different account types**. |

---

## What does an auctioneer do on Auction Journal?

### Set up the business

Before and between sales, auctioneers configure how Auction Journal should work for them, for example:

- **Profile** — company name, bio, website, and photo ([update profile](profile.md))
- **Miscellaneous** — formulas (buyer premium, tax, commission), chart of accounts, invoice details, reusable templates, Stripe Connect to receive payments from bidders
- **Payment card on file** — for platform services such as publishing [listings](../listing/index.md) or [advertisements](../advertisement/index.md) ([payment method](payment-method.md))

### Run sales

Typical auctioneer work on the platform includes:

| Activity | What it means for you |
|----------|------------------------|
| **Listings** | Advertise property or assets for sale (often before or alongside an auction). |
| **Auctions** | Create timed or live sales, add **lots**, set terms and notices, open registration for bidders. |
| **During the sale** | **Clerk** lots (record bids and results) and manage the event, including live webcast auctions where supported. |
| **After the sale** | **Settlement** — invoices, buyer/seller totals, and payment follow-up. |
| **Customers** | Maintain seller and buyer records, mailing lists, and floor bidders tied to your company. |

### Reach bidders and the public

- Your auctions and listings appear on the **public Auction Journal** site so bidders can search, register, and bid.
- You can add **videos** and **blogs** to promote your business and sales.
- **Advertisements** can highlight your brand or events on the platform.

### Work with your team

- **Users** (assistants) can be invited to help operate the dashboard on your behalf. See [How do I use the Auctioneer Dashboard?](dashboard.md).
