# What should I do if I forget my login password?

Use **Forgot Password** on the Auctioneer Dashboard when you cannot sign in. You will reset your password in **two parts**: request a link by email, then set a new password from that link.

---

## Step 1 — Request a reset link

1. On the login page, open **Forgot Password** (or go to the forgot-password page for auctioneers).
2. Enter the **email address** you used when you registered your auctioneer account.
3. Select **Submit**.
4. If the email is recognized, you will see a message that a message was sent to that address. **Open your inbox** (and spam/junk if needed).

Use only an email that is **already registered** on Auction Journal. If you never finished auctioneer signup, fix registration first or contact support.

---

## Step 2 — Open the email link

1. Find the **reset password** email from Auction Journal.
2. Click the **link** in the email. It opens the site in your browser and takes you to the **reset password** screen.
3. If the link does not work or says it **expired**, go back to Step 1 and request a new link.

---

## Step 3 — Choose a new password

1. On the reset screen, enter a **new password** and type it again under **confirm password**.
2. Use a **strong** password: at least **8** characters with **uppercase and lowercase letters**, a **number**, and a **special character**, as the form requires.
3. Select **Submit**.
4. When you see confirmation that the password was reset, **sign in** with your email and the **new** password.

---

## If something goes wrong

- **Email has not registered** — The address is not on file as a completed auctioneer account. Check for typos or use **Sign Up** if you are new.
- **No email arrived** — Wait a few minutes, check spam, and try **Forgot Password** again.
- **Expired link** — Request a new reset email from Step 1.
- Reset page will not load — Use the **latest** link from your most recent email; old links stop working after they expire.
