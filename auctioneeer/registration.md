# How do I register in Auction Journal as an auctioneer?

This guide walks you through **Auctioneer Sign Up** from start to finish. Registration happens in **three steps** on one form. You will need a few things ready before you begin.

---

## Before you start

Have the following available:

- A **valid email address** you can open during signup (you will use it for login and for a verification message after you finish).
- A **U.S. mobile phone number** that can receive **text messages** (SMS), including country code **+1**. Landlines and numbers that cannot receive texts will not work for verification.
- Your **company name**, **company logo** as an image file, **auctioneer license number**, and your business **website**.
- Optionally, a **photo or scan** of your license document (image file), if you want to attach it.
- Your **mailing address** (street, ZIP, city, state, country).

---

## Step 1 of 3 — Your name and address

1. Open the **Auctioneer Sign Up** page on Auction Journal (the page title usually shows **AUCTIONEER SIGN UP** and a progress indicator with steps **1, 2, 3**).
2. Read the note that required fields are marked with an asterisk (**\***).
3. Enter your **first name** and **last name**.
4. Enter your **street address** (full address line).
5. Enter your **ZIP code**. If the form fills in **city**, **state**, or **country** for you, check that they are correct and fix any mistakes.
6. Confirm **city**, **state**, and **country** are accurate.
7. Select **Next** to go to step 2.

You cannot move forward until the required fields on this screen are valid.

---

## Step 2 of 3 — Your business and license

1. Enter your **company name**.
2. Under **company logo**, choose an **image** file from your device. This is required and should represent your business.
3. Enter your **auctioneer license number** exactly as it appears on your credentials.
4. If the form offers **upload license** (optional), you may attach an **image** of your license. Skip this if you prefer; only attach a file if it is a clear image.
5. Enter your **website** (the address people use to find your business online).
6. Select **Next** to go to step 3.

---

## Step 3 of 3 — Phone, email, password, and agreements

### Verify your phone number

1. Enter your mobile number using **+1** followed by your **10-digit** U.S. number (the form is built for this format).
2. Select **GET OTP**. A one-time code will be sent by **text message** to that number.
3. When the code arrives, enter it in the **OTP** field.
4. Select **VERIFY OTP**. If the code is wrong or expired, correct it or select **RESEND OTP** to get a new code, then verify again.
5. Wait until the form shows that your number is **Verified** (you may see a checkmark or “Verified” next to the phone field). You must reach this state before you can complete signup.

### Email and password

1. Enter your **email address**, then enter the same address again in **confirm email**. Use **lowercase** letters in your email, as the form may require it.
2. Choose a **password** and enter it again in **confirm password**. Your password must be **strong**: long enough and include **uppercase and lowercase letters**, at least one **number**, and at least one **special character** (such as symbols on your keyboard). The form will tell you if the password does not meet the rules.

### Agreements and submit

1. Read the **Terms and Conditions** and check the box to confirm you agree. This is required.
2. If you see options about **newsletters** or updates, read each line and check the boxes according to what you want. The signup flow may **require** you to opt in to the Auction Journal update newsletter to submit—if submit is blocked, ensure that required box is checked.
3. Select **Submit** (or the final button on this step) and wait until the process finishes. Do not close the page while it is saving.

---

## After you submit

- You should see a **success** confirmation on the site.
- **Check your email** for a message from Auction Journal. You may need to **verify your email address** by following a link in that message before certain features work fully.
- Additional **dashboard setup** (payment, invoice, clients, and similar) may be required before you can run auctions or use every feature. See your onboarding or help materials for “initial setup” if offered.

---

## If something goes wrong

- **“Phone number already registered”** — That number is already used for an auctioneer account. Use a different number or sign in instead of registering again.
- **“Email is already registered”** — That email is taken. Use another email or use **forgot password** if this is your account.
- **OTP problems** — Request a new code with **RESEND OTP**, confirm the number is correct, and make sure texts are not blocked on your phone.
- **Submit errors about the phone** — Complete **VERIFY OTP** first so the form shows your number as verified.

If you are stuck, use **[Help and Support](../help-and-support/index.md)** to reach Auction Journal support.
