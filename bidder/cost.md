# What does it cost to become a bidder in Auction Journal?

**Nothing.** Auction Journal does **not** charge a fee to **sign up** as a bidder or to complete **verified bidder** setup.

---

## Free on Auction Journal

| What you do | Platform charge |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Bidder registration** (name, address, phone, email, password) | **$0** — see [How do I register as a bidder?](registration.md) |
| **Become a verified bidder** (upload ID + save a card) | **$0** — see [How to become a verified bidder?](verification.md) |

---

## About the card during verification

When you become a **verified bidder**, you add a **credit or debit card** through **Stripe**. That step is **not** a payment to Auction Journal to open your account. The card is kept on file so you can meet auction rules (many auctions require a verified bidder with a saved payment method).

You are only charged later when an **auction’s own rules** apply — for example if you **win lots** and owe hammer price, buyer’s premium, or taxes. Those amounts come from the **auction and auctioneer**, not from “joining” Auction Journal.
