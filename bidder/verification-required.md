# Is it mandatory to become a verified bidder? What are the benefits?

**Short answer:** You do **not** have to become verified just to **create a bidder account**. Many auctions **do** require **verified bidder** status before you can **register for that sale**. Completing verification is still worth it for access, trust, and faster registration on those auctions.

---

## Is verification mandatory?

| Situation | Do you need verified bidder status? |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| **Signing up** on Auction Journal | **No** — complete [bidder registration](registration.md) first. |
| **Registering for a specific auction** | **Depends on the auction** — the auctioneer chooses the registration type. |
| **Most auctions (default setting)** | **Yes** — registration type is usually **Verified Bidder**. You need a saved **card** and **Driving Licence or State ID** on file, then **verified** status. |
| **Auctions set to Registered Bidder only** | **No full verified flow** for that rule — but you still need a valid **ID upload** on your account to register, per the site prompts. |

If you try to register for a **Verified Bidder** auction without finishing verification, Auction Journal will ask you to go to **Verified Bidder** in your dashboard and complete the steps in [How to become a verified bidder?](verification.md).

---

## Benefits of verification

| Benefit | What it means for you |
|---------|------------------------|
| **Join more auctions** | Register for sales that require **Verified Bidder** (the common default). |
| **Smoother registration** | Product messaging describes **instant** or faster approval when you are verified and the auction expects it. |
| **One-time setup** | Add your card and ID once; reuse for future verified-bidder auctions (you can update card or ID later). |
| **Bidder score** | Verification adds to your **bidder score** (trust signal for auctioneers). |
| **Payment method ready** | Verified-bidder auction registration can use your saved card when the auctioneer requires it. |
| **Security** | Card is processed by **Stripe**; ID helps confirm bidders are real. |

---

## How to get verified

Follow [How to become a verified bidder?](verification.md) — **Verified Bidder** in the bidder menu → card (Step 1) → ID (Step 2).
