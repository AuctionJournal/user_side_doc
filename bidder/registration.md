# How do I register in Auction Journal as a bidder?

This guide walks you through **Bidder Sign Up** on the public Auction Journal site. Registration has **two steps** on one flow. Have your details ready before you start.

---

## Before you start

- A **valid email address** you can open (you will use it to sign in and you may need to **verify** it from a message after signup).
- A **U.S. mobile phone number** that can receive **SMS**, with country code **+1** and ten digits after it. Use a number that can receive texts, not a landline.
- Your **home or mailing address** (you will enter street, ZIP, city, state; **country** is set to **USA** on the form).

---

## Step 1 of 2 — Your name and address

1. Open **Bidder Sign Up** (the page shows **BIDDER SIGN UP** and a progress line with **1** and **2**; step **1** is highlighted).
2. Read *Please enter the information correctly* — fields marked **\*** are required.
3. Enter **first name** and **last name**.
4. Enter your **address** in the large address box (street, building, unit as needed).
5. Enter **ZIP**. If **city** or **state** fill in automatically, check them and correct if needed.
6. **Country** shows **USA**; you usually do not change it on this screen.
7. Select **Next** to go to step 2.

---

## Step 2 of 2 — Phone, email, password, and agreements

### Verify your phone

1. Enter your mobile number with **+1** and your **10-digit** number (only as many digits as the form allows).
2. Select **GET OTP**. Wait for a **text message** with a code.
3. When the OTP fields appear, enter the code and select **VERIFY OTP**. Use **RESEND OTP** if you need a new code.
4. Wait until you see **Verified** next to your number before you submit.

### Email and password

1. Enter your **email**, then the same address under **confirm email**.
2. Choose a **password** and enter it again under **confirm password**. Use a **strong** password: at least **8** characters with **uppercase and lowercase letters**, a **number**, and a **special character**, as the form requires.

### Agreements and submit

1. Read and check **Terms and Conditions** (required).
2. Optional: check **featured auctions** notices if you want those updates.
3. To finish signup, the site expects you to **opt in to the Auction Journal update Newsletter** (check that box). If **Submit** fails, confirm this box is checked.
4. Select **Submit** and wait until registration completes.

---

## After you submit

- You should see a **thank you** message asking you to **check your email** to verify your email address.
- Follow the link in that email when it arrives (check spam if you do not see it).
- **Verified bidder** status is a **separate** step after signup — see [How to become a verified bidder?](verification.md). Signup costs **nothing** — [What does it cost to become a bidder?](cost.md).

---

## If something goes wrong

- **Phone number already registered** — Sign in instead, or use a different phone if appropriate.
- **OTP** — Use **RESEND OTP**, double-check the number, and ensure texts are not blocked.
- **Email already registered** — Use another email or **forgot password** if it is your account.
- **Please verify mobile number** — Complete **VERIFY OTP** until you see **Verified**.
