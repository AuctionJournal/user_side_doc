# What should I do if I forget my login password?

Use **Forgot Password** on the Auction Journal website when you cannot sign in as a **bidder**. You will reset your password in **two parts**: request a link by email, then set a new password from that link.

---

## Step 1 — Request a reset link

1. On the bidder **login** page, open **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter the **email address** tied to your bidder account.
3. Select **Submit**.
4. When the site confirms that email was sent, **check your inbox** (and spam/junk folder).

The email must already be registered as a bidder on Auction Journal.

---

## Step 2 — Open the email link

1. Open the **reset password** message from Auction Journal.
2. Click the **link** in the email. Your browser should open the **reset password** page.
3. If the link fails or is **expired**, return to Step 1 and request a new link.

---

## Step 3 — Choose a new password

1. Enter your **new password** and the same password again under **confirm password**.
2. Follow the form’s strength rules (length, upper and lower case, number, and special character).
3. Select **Submit**.
4. After success, go to **Login** and sign in with your email and **new** password.

---

## If something goes wrong

- **Email has not registered** — No bidder account uses that address; check spelling or **Sign Up** if you are new.
- **No email** — Wait, check spam, try Forgot Password again.
- **Expired link** — Start over from Step 1.
- Page asks for login instead of reset — Open only the link from your **most recent** reset email.
