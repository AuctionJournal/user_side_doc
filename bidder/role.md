# Who is a bidder? What does a bidder do in Auction Journal?

A **bidder** is a **person** (or buyer acting for themselves) who uses Auction Journal to **find auctions**, **register to bid**, and **compete for lots** run by auctioneers on the platform.

Bidders use the **public website** to browse sales and a **Bidder Dashboard** after sign-in to track registrations, bids, and account details.

---

## Who is a bidder?

| Idea | In plain terms |
|------|----------------|
| **Who** | Anyone who wants to **buy** at auctions hosted on Auction Journal—not the auction company running the sale. |
| **Account** | Your own bidder profile (name, address, phone, email, password). |
| **Not an auctioneer account** | Auctioneers run sales; bidders participate in them. You need a **bidder** registration to bid, even if you also work in the auction industry elsewhere. |

---

## What does a bidder do on Auction Journal?

### Discover sales

On the public site you can:

- **Search** for auctions and lots
- View auction details, terms, notices, and listing information
- Read auctioneer profiles, blogs, and videos

### Join a sale

| Step | What you do |
|------|-------------|
| **Register on Auction Journal** | Create a free bidder account ([registration](registration.md) — [no signup fee](cost.md)). |
| **Become a verified bidder** (when required) | Many auctions ask for **Verified Bidder** status: save a card and upload ID once ([verification](verification.md), [why it matters](verification-required.md)). |
| **Register for an auction** | For a specific sale, submit registration so the auctioneer can approve you to bid (terms and details vary per auction). |

### Bid and follow your activity

After you are approved for an auction, you can **place bids** on lots (timed online, live webcast, or other formats the auctioneer offers). In your **Bidder Dashboard** you can typically:

- See **auctions** you registered for and your **bids**
- Use a **watchlist** and **lot alerts**
- Review **permissions** the auctioneer set for you
- Check **invoices** and payment-related information when settlements apply
- View your **bidder score** history (a trust signal auctioneers may use)

### Manage your account

- Update [profile details](profile.md) (name, address, phone)
- Change your password or use [forgot password](forgot-password.md)
- Manage verification (card and ID) on **Verified Bidder**
- Get help via [Help and Support](../help-and-support/index.md)

---

## What a bidder does *not* do on Auction Journal

Bidders do **not** create auctions, clerk lots, run settlement for a sale, or configure an auctioneer’s formulas and Stripe Connect—that is the **auctioneer’s** role ([who is an auctioneer?](../auctioneeer/role.md)).

---

## How do I get started?

1. [Register as a bidder](registration.md)  
2. When needed, complete [verified bidder](verification.md) setup  
3. Browse auctions on Auction Journal and register for sales you want to join  
4. Use the [Bidder Dashboard](dashboard.md) to track bids, alerts, and account details
