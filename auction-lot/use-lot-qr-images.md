# How to use lot QR images

Last modified: 2026-05-28

A lot QR image is a quick link to that lot's page on `auctionjournal.com`.

When someone scans the lot QR code, it opens the related lot page directly in the browser.

---

## What happens when you scan

1. Open a phone camera (or QR scanner app).
2. Point it at the lot QR image/label.
3. Tap the detected link.
4. The browser opens that lot page in Auction Journal.

This helps users jump to the exact lot without manually searching the auction.

---

## Where this is useful

- Lot tags on-site
- Printed lot sheets
- Catalog handouts
- Warehouse/inventory labels

---

## Tips for reliable scanning

- Keep the QR image clear and not blurry.
- Avoid very small print size.
- Ensure enough light while scanning.
- Keep lot labels clean (no folds over the QR).
