# How do I highlight my listing for better bidder reach?

The way to give a **published listing** more visibility on Auction Journal is to **create a paid advertisement** for it—not a separate “highlight” button on the listing itself.

## What to do

1. Open your Auctioneer Dashboard.
2. Go to **Advertise With Us** (`/dashboard/advertisement`).
3. Choose **Advertise your Listings**.
4. Pick an ad type for that listing:
   - **Ticker Symbol** — listing appears in the site header ticker.
   - **Featured Listing (National)** — featured placement on national listing discovery.
   - **Featured Listing (State)** — featured placement on that state’s listing views.
5. Select the **listing** you want to promote.
6. Choose your **duration** (and **state**, for state featured ads).
7. Complete **payment** at checkout.

After payment, the ad runs for the dates you selected and the listing is promoted on the public site for that package.

## Before you advertise

- The listing should already be **published** (you select from published listings in the ad flow).
- Advertisement packages are **paid** by duration. See [How does Advertisement work?](../advertisement/how-it-works.md).
